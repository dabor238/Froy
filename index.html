What is Flux Adventures?

Flux Adventures is not only a guiding service and climbing school but also a way to share our climbing passion with whomever has the desire to explore and look for new adventures in their lives.

We provide the tools to make the rock climbing activities safe and fun, while also providing the best experience possible and the ability to enjoy nature at the same time. Currently we offer our services throughout the United States, but we focus our services in Joshua Tree National Park and Devils Tower. We also have exciting climbs to share with you in Ecuador, Colombia and Per√∫. In any destination you chose, we want to adapt to your needs and make the best customize day or trip for you. Flux Adventures is also a way to share our trips and adventures with other people and invite them to explore new places, look for new adventures and pursue a healthy life by promoting outdoor sports like mountaineering, rock climbing, hiking and biking.
