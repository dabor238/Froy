Ropes and anchors systems course

If you are interested in acquiring skills to make your climbs safer and more efficient- increase your knowledge about anchors and rope management. This courses will provide you with all the tools needed to climb safely through all terrains. We offer courses from Top Roping to Big Wall techniques, depending on the level you wish to work on. Self-rescue techniques are also a part of this course. Get certified now! If you want to pursue the guiding world, you can also get a PCGI certification at Top Rope or Single Pitch level. Courses are offered from a single full day up to five days. Prices will change depending on the number of days and people. If you are interested contact us via e-mail or phone.
